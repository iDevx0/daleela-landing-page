Frequently asked questions

Your questions answered

What is the Daleela app?
Daleela is a health platform designed to support women's health. It includes an AI health assistant, educational programs, diagnostic consultations with certified doctors, and a period tracker.
Who can use Daleela?
Daleela is designed for Arabic-speaking women aged 18 and over. It offers support on reproductive health, menstrual health, fertility, and wellness.
Is Daleela free to use?
Yes. You can download and use Daleela for free. Some features, such as diagnostic consultations and advanced programs, are available through subscriptions or one-time purchase.
How does Daleela work as an AI assistant?
Daleela is an AI health assistant trained to answer your reproductive-health questions. Subscribers get unlimited use with tailored answers. The free version includes a limited number of questions per month with basic support.
Are there limits on chatting with Daleela?
Yes. The free version allows a limited number of questions per month. Subscribers have unlimited use and can ask as many questions as they need with full, tailored answers.
What kind of questions can I ask Daleela?
You can ask about women's sexual health, period tracking and cycle health, contraception, fertility and pregnancy, mental health related to women's health, and general health topics.
What topics does Daleela avoid?
Daleela is designed to avoid topics that depend on cultural or personal beliefs. Some questions are referred to a doctor for direct consultation.
What if Daleela doesn't understand my question?
Daleela will ask you to rephrase your question. If it can't help directly, it will guide you to a certified doctor consultation or suggest in-app resources such as diagnostic consultations, articles, programs, or tools.
Is Daleela available 24/7?
Yes. Daleela is always available to answer your questions.
Does Daleela give personalised advice?
Yes. Daleela gives personalised advice based on the information you provide. It does not replace professional medical advice.
What do the educational programs cover?
Daleela offers content on menstrual health, contraception, sexual health, nutrition, and more, created by doctors and specialists.
Do educational programs expire?
No. Once you buy a program, the content stays available to you permanently.
What content is in the educational programs?
Programs include video lessons from experts, simple visuals, podcasts, and articles on a wide range of topics.
Are the programs suitable for all levels?
Yes. The programs are designed for beginners and those with prior knowledge, covering basics and advanced information.
Can I buy programs without an annual subscription?
Yes. You can buy programs individually without a subscription.
How does the diagnostic feature work?
Choose a consultation topic that matches your concern and describe your symptoms. Answer the tailored follow-up questions. A certified doctor will review your case within 24 hours and may ask for more details or recommend tests. You then receive a detailed report and treatment plan, and can ask follow-up questions.
What conditions does the diagnostic feature cover?
It covers vaginal discharge and infections, sexual health, contraception, skin and hair issues, fertility, and egg freezing.
What is not suitable for the diagnostic feature?
Conditions that need an in-person exam, emergencies, pregnancy complications, and chronic conditions requiring ongoing care are not suitable for remote diagnosis.
Who reviews diagnostic cases?
All cases are reviewed by certified doctors to ensure accurate medical guidance.
How long until I get a diagnostic response?
You get a response within 24 hours. Complex cases may take longer.
Can I ask follow-up questions after a diagnosis?
Yes. You can ask follow-up questions about your case.
How does the diagnostic feature protect my privacy?
All information is encrypted and reviewed in strict confidence by certified doctors. Your data is never shared without your explicit consent.
When is the period tracker available?
The period tracker is now available in the app.
Are there topics Daleela avoids?
Daleela avoids topics that depend on culture or personal beliefs. Some questions can only be answered by a doctor. Daleela aims to provide a safe, respectful space for everyone.
Can Daleela diagnose a specific health condition?
Daleela itself does not diagnose or treat. It asks guided questions and refers your case to a certified doctor. When suitable for remote care, the doctor provides diagnosis and treatment options. When in-person care is needed, Daleela will advise you to see a doctor.
Can my partner use Daleela too?
Daleela is designed for women's health, but much of the content is useful for couples. You can share relevant sections with your partner to support understanding and communication.
What are the subscription options?
You can subscribe annually for unlimited AI assistant use, full access to all programs, and up to 12 diagnostic consultations per year. You can also buy programs or consultations individually without a subscription.
How can I pay for a subscription, consultation, or program?
You can pay easily with debit or credit cards via Google Pay and Apple Pay. Users in Egypt can also use e-wallets.
What happens if I cancel my subscription?
When you cancel, premium features stop at the end of your paid period. Programs and consultations you bought separately remain available.
Are there discounts for new users?
Promotional discounts for new users may be available. Check our Instagram or the Subscriptions section in the app or website for current offers.
Can I switch from pay-per-use to an annual subscription?
Yes. You can switch to an annual subscription at any time to get full access to all features.
How do I check my subscription details?
You can see your subscription type and end date in the Account section of the app.
My subscription isn't working. What should I do?
Confirm that payment succeeded and restart the app. If the issue continues, contact support and provide your transaction ID.
How can I update my profile?
In the Account section of the app you can edit your name, email, and phone number.
Can I delete my account?
Yes. You can delete your account, but this permanently removes all your data. Contact support to complete the process.
Is my data safe with Daleela?
Yes. Daleela follows strict security and data protection standards and complies with privacy regulations.
Can I delete my chat history with Daleela?
Yes. You can delete chat history in the app settings.
How is my health information used?
Your data is used to give you personalised recommendations and improve our services. No data is shared without your explicit consent.
How can I contact support?
You can reach support via in-app chat, email, phone, or WhatsApp. Details are in the Help section of the app.
What if I need help with a topic Daleela doesn't cover?
Daleela will guide you to a certified doctor consultation for topics outside the app's scope.
Can I contact doctors directly?
No. To protect privacy and for operational reasons, we no longer share doctors' direct contact details.
